Coflexip & Flexible Test Lines

Comprehensive Summary (FOH & POM)

This summary covers the guidelines for Approved Suppliers, Preparation, Rig-up, Operations, and Maintenance of Flexible Test Lines.

1. Approved Suppliers and Types

SLB Testing has qualified three specific suppliers:

  • Coflexip (Technip): Generally designed for Sour Service (H2S), except for specific models.
  • Contitech: Well Test Production Hoses designed for Sour Service (H2S).
  • Black Eagle (Parker): Designed for STANDARD SERVICE ONLY (Non-H2S).
    • Does not comply with API 6A.
    • Restricted for use downstream of Choke Manifold or Kill Line.
    • Must be clearly marked "STANDARD SERVICE ONLY / NON H2S".

2. Preparation and Transportation

  • Inspection: Check carcass attachment, sheath protection, and internal ends before load out.
  • Storage/Transport: Use a standard Coflex basket. Ensure basket dimensions allow the hose to maintain its Minimum Bending Radius (MBR).
  • Handling Best Practices:
    • Do NOT attach crossovers during transport.
    • Maintain a straight length of 0.6 to 0.9 m (2 to 3 feet) immediately behind the end-fitting.
    • Never drag the bottom end over the deck.

3. Rig-Up and Installation

  • Lifting: Use certified lifting clamps and slings. Do not lift by end connections alone.
  • Marking: Paint a white mark (tally mark) across the connection to identify back-off.
  • Securing the Hose:
    • Vertical (Preferred): Hang vertically. Secure with wire rope to take weight off the union.
    • Horizontal: Use a lifting strap approx. 2.5m (8 ft) from the end connection supported by an air hoist/chain block. The connection must not take the hose weight.
  • Connections: API or Grayloc hubs are strongly recommended over Hammer Unions (saver subs carry higher leakage risk).
  • Swaging: Must be swaged directly. Intermediate threads are prohibited.

4. Operational Limits and Safety

  • Bending Radius: Strict adherence to MBR is mandatory during all phases.
  • Flow Velocity Limits:
    • Coflexip: Max 15 m/s (49 ft/s).
    • Contitech: Max 15 m/s (liquid), 20 m/s (dry gas), 8 m/s (gaseous oil).
    • Black Eagle: Max 30 m/s (non-abrasive mono-phase).
  • Temperature: Respect max working temp vs exposure time.
  • Chemicals: Verify compatibility (e.g., standard Coflexip is not for acidified effluent).

5. Maintenance and Certification

  • Service Level 1 (TRIM): Every job. Inspection + Pressure Test to WP.
  • Service Level 2 (Annual): Every 12 months. Full inspection + Pressure Test.
  • Service Level 3 (Major Survey): Every 5 years by manufacturer/3rd party.
  • Lifespan: Max service life is 20 years. Older hoses must be junked.
  • Traceability: Must maintain records of H2S, CO2, Acid, Pressure, and Temp exposure.

⚠️ Safety Critical Points

  • Black Eagle Hoses: STRICTLY Non-H2S / Standard Service only.
  • Bending Radius: Never exceed the Minimum Bending Radius (MBR).
  • Tension: Do not allow end connections (unions) to bear the weight of the hose.
  • Swaging: No intermediate threads allowed.
